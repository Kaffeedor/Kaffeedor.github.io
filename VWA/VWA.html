-Research question: "What are the current state-of-the-art techniques used in the search for extraterrestrial life, and what are the limitations and challenges of this research?"

3. "The mathematical foundations of radio astronomy." This could involve researching the various mathematical principles and techniques that are used in the field, such as signal processing and data analysis.
-Research question: "What are the key mathematical concepts and techniques that are used in radio astronomy, and how do they enable us to study the universe?"

-Research question: "How do radio astronomy observations help us to test fundamental theories of physics, and what are the challenges and limitations of this approach?"

-Research question: "What can radio telescopes tell us about the early stages of the universe, and how do these observations help us to better understand the origins of the universe?"
